Smart Tenders Overview

Smart Tenders feature is part of the app that allows hundreds of dealers, and thousands of users to request jobs required for their vehicles. The Supplier has the opportunity to win customers loyalty with strong offers. Once your bid is selected you will have the chance to upsell them on various products, if they are happy with your level of quality and pricing. When a client chooses a company, they will base their decision on the pricing and star rating. It is paramount to maintain a high rating through quality and good customer service.If the customer chooses another supplier, you will be informed of the offer chosen in order to be able to assess how you could improve your future offers. The Alba Cars platform will eliminate the need for expensive marketing campaigns.

SMART TENDERS
RECOVERY

This state of the art feature enables the client to select both vehile 'Pick Up' and 'Drop Off' locations from a digital map. You may even select the type of recovery, wether if be flatbed or standard.

Once a tender is posted, there is an opportunity to make an offer to the client. Your excellent high standards and competitive prices on the platform will increase your number of loyal clients, who will in turn contact you in the future.

SMART TENDERS
VEHICLE INSPECTION

An opportunity arises when a client trusts you to conduct an inspection on their vehicle. If the client wishes to proceed with the purchase of the vehicle and your inspection was fair, it is highly likely that they will conduct any future repair work at your garage.

This will increase footfall of potential clients in your facility, and as a result the number of loyal customers will grow.

SMART TENDERS
CAR SERVICING

Appealing car servicing deals are highly likely to attract further work such as repairs and upgrades.

Offering attractive car servicing deals increases the number of clients visiting your workshop. If customer service is of a high standard, then customer retention will follow.

Once you receive a 'Service Request' notification, simply make an agreeable offer and clients will choose you as their preffered supplier.

SMART TENDERS
UPHOLSTERY

Many people are unaware of the number of car interior enhancements your company provides. This app showcases the numerous services to thousands of app users, and encourages them to visit your facility.

A job request with photographs of the necessary work will be sent to you. A bid must submitted within the specified time frame, and the offer must be good enough to beat the competition.

SMART TENDERS
SPARE PARTS

Receive daily requests for parts from car owners and dealers. This portal is designed for end users who prefer to cut out the middleman, in order to acquire better prices or increased profits.

Notifications for specific car brands of which you specialise in will appear. Make an offer that's suitable for you.

Delivery options are available as an additional means of revenue.

SMART TENDERS
WHEELS

Clients tend to spend vast amounts of money on wheels and tyres when dealing with showrooms. This app presents a link between you and the car owner.

If competitive prices and good customer service are provided, a loyal customer base will follow without the need of expensive marketing or advertising.

This platform enables you to receive requests from clients wishing to purchase wheels and tyres. Make a bid in order to win the clients over.

SMART TENDERS
BODY WORK

Workshop business generally centres around bodywork and paint. This app will provide access to a number of daily requests from clients who require such work.

There is a further opportunity to sell the client other services which their vehicle may require.

A bid request with photographs of the necessary work will be sent. A bid must be submitted within the specified time frame, and the other offer must be good enough to beat the competition.

SMART TENDERS
CAR DETAILING

High rating and good pricing on this app will guarantee clients through your door.

Requests will be sent from clients and dealers who are in the market searching for new suppliers. This lucrative opportunity provides a way to put forward an offer to attract customers.

SMART TENDERS
WINDOW TINTING

This feature acquires window tinting requests. A detailed request including the percentage of tinting will be sent. In return, you may place an offer mentioning the brand and quality used.

If your offer is the most competitive the customer will choose you as their preffered supplier.
