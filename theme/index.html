Smart Tenders Overview

Smart Tenders feature is part of the app that allows hundreds of dealers, and thousands of users to request jobs required for their vehicles.

SMART TENDERS
RECOVERY

This state of the art feature enables the client to select both vehile 'Pick Up' and 'Drop Off' locations from a digital map. You may even select the type of recovery, wether if be flatbed or standard.

Once a tender is posted, there is an opportunity to make an offer to the client. Your excellent high standards and competitive prices on the platform will increase your number of loyal clients, who will in turn contact you in the future.

SMART TENDERS
VEHICLE INSPECTION

An opportunity arises when a client trusts you to conduct an inspection on their vehicle. If the client wishes to proceed with the purchase of the vehicle and your inspection was fair, it is highly likely that they will conduct any future repair work at your garage.

This will increase footfall of potential clients in your facility, and as a result the number of loyal customers will grow.

SMART TENDERS
CAR SERVICING

Appealing car servicing deals are highly likely to attract further work such as repairs and upgrades.

Offering attractive car servicing deals increases the number of clients visiting your workshop. If customer service is of a high standard, then customer retention will follow.

Once you receive a 'Service Request' notification, simply make an agreeable offer and clients will choose you as their preffered supplier.

SMART TENDERS
UPHOLSTERY

Many people are unaware of the number of car interior enhancements your company provides. This app showcases the numerous services to thousands of app users, and encourages them to visit your facility.

A job request with photographs of the necessary work will be sent to you. A bid must submitted within the specified time frame, and the offer must be good enough to beat the competition.
