Dealers by Alba

Dealers are often looking for ways to save cost and time on preparation, as well as find new sources to buy and sell their stock. The app provides a portal to fulfil all, as well as a marketplace of customers to buy and sell to. They may inspect, buy, prepare, sell, purchase warranty and profit from their deals, all from within the app.

Overview

AUCTIONS
SELL

Create an auction room that allows hundreds of dealers to bid and purchase cars you wish to sell. Doing so ensures you get the highest possible price for a car that you may not wish to retail.

Simply post the vehicle you wish to sell and dealers will bid on a live auction platform, of which you choose when to run and for how long.

Once you recieve the highest bid there is an option to accept or reject.

Additionaly you will have peace of mind knowing that we have installed strict app policies which ban any dealers who have a track record of failing to commit to won an auctions.

AUCTIONS
BUY

Gain access to a large number of auction rooms run by several dealers and showrooms. This is a great method to increase your stock and compete towards a whole new market that may have been available to you.

All vehicles are posted with a complete set of photos, as well as a comprehensive visual inspection report posted by the auctioneer.

Once you win a car in auction, the payment is safely kept at ALBA CARS, providing you with a 24 hour window to inspect the vehicle. If the inspection does not match what was posted you will be entitled to request for a refund.

FLEET

With an ever growing market of dealers, the need for quality and profitable stock increases.

Purchasing a car from an individual who focuses on how much they have lost makes the negotiating an arduous experience; however, purchasing a car from a company who is calculating their profits based on using the vehicle for business purposes, increases the likelihood of getting a better deal.

This section of the app gives access to a huge number of fleet vehicles posted by companies that wish to sell them after use; these include a car lease companies and more.

SMART TENDERS
OVERVIEW

This feature acts as a useful portal in providing you with all your car preparation needs.

A vast number of services are available to you, simply post the job required and star rated suppliers will put forward competitive offers for you to choose from.

Competing suppliers will be made aware of the offer you have chosen. This will ensure that offers are continuously improved to save you cost.

LEADS
LOOKING TO BUY

Imagine having to access to a wide pool of client contact details requesting for the purchase of specific cars.

This segment allows you to directly and instantly connect with those clients who are interested in specific cars, this means you can sell newly purchased stock prior to incurring costs on advertising.

If your car meets the client's requirements as posted. you may sell the vehicle faster than ever before.

LEADS
LOOKING TO SELL

This feature presents a whole new method of sourcing vehicles. Many customers may not wish to pay advertising fees to sell their cars, and are happier to deal with traders for convenience sake.

This segment is a database of customers selling their vehicles, simply shortlist your desired car, contact the client and make an offer. In addition, you can choose to make the lead exclusive, meaning the lead will no longer be available for other dealers to contact.

WARRANTY & SERVICE
CONTRACTS

A great way of protecting yourself and the client is to issue warranty on the vehicle sold. This add on will not only protect your client financially, but also protects your reputation as a dealer, as you are able to guarantee the repair of defects after sale.

Service contracts provide additional piece of mind and make your car a more attractive deal to the end user.

Given the fair prices provided, this can become an additional mode of revenue while maintaning a fair price of sale to the end user.

SERVICE
HISTORY

Are you tired of making long trips and waiting in queue to retrieve the service history of a car you wish to buy or sell? This process could also lead to a loss of deals, due to the time taken and inconvenience.

This feature will directly send your request to the dealership, who will in turn send the history information required, all from the comfort of your home or workspace.
